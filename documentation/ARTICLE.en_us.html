Zalando Sans is not just a typeface; it is a defining cornerstone of the Zalando brand identity. This highly versatile variable font family acts as a bold, expressive feature, reflecting our modern brand essence. It is designed to adapt seamlessly across diverse tones and styles, allowing us to meet varied needs across different categories and audiences with precision and accessibility at its heart.

Staying true to Zalando’s core characteristics—Bold, Fun, and Welcoming—Zalando Sans elevates every design it inhabits. When used effectively, it becomes a powerful, recognizable element that will be closely associated with the brand, reinforcing its commitment to helping people feel confident in their style.

To contribute, see github.com/zalando/sans

The Zalando Sans family offers exceptional adaptability through its variable font technology. This flexibility allows for a wide range of expressive possibilities while maintaining a cohesive brand presence. Its clean lines and modern aesthetic reflect Zalando's forward-thinking approach to fashion and lifestyle.

The Zalando Sans typeface family is central to Zalando's elevated core design elements, crafting a flexible yet cohesive identity and marking its evolution into an inspiring destination for fashion, lifestyle, and entertainment.

With a weight and width axes, the typeface embodies Zalando’s key design principles:

  • Simple: An intuitive and easy-to-use system, ensuring a consistent experience.
  • Dynamic: Enhanced brand assets that drive recognition and pride. Consistent: A clarified brand role that fosters strong associations.
  • Flexible: Future-ready design, built to scale with new propositions, experiences, and channels.
